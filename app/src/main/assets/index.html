Episode

User Manual

Sign In or Sign Up

This is the main activity. This is what you will see when you first launch the app. To sign up, click the google button in the middle of the page. Episode will only store a long unique number that is associated with your Google account.

Discover

In this screen, you will be able to see the most trending, popular, most played and most saved T.V. shows. You can add any show to your collection by pressing the + button in the bottom right of each show poster. To go to the show page, click on the show poster. You can switch between the Watch List, Discover and My Account pages with the navigation tabs at the top of the page. You could also swipe across the whole page.

Search

Tapping on the search bar pops up the keyboard and you can then type in your initial search query. On pressing enter or the blue search icon in the keyboard, it will launch the search page. You can go to the show page by tapping on the show poster. Once on the search page, you can continue to type and the results will change as you type.

Show Details

This page shows a synopsis of the plot of the show. Then the next section down displays when the show started, what network it airs on, if it has ended and the rating. Below that is a list of all the episodes for that show. You can add the show to your collection by pressing the button in the top right corner. With the same button, you can remove the show too. To mark an episode as watched you can press the toggle switch on the right side of each episode. To make an episode as not watched you can press the toggle again. To move between the details and comment pages, use the tab navigation that's at the top of the page. You could also swipe across the whole page.

Show Comments

This page shows the most popular comments about the show. This will help you decide if the show is worth watching.

Watch List

Once you have added shows to your collection you can see the next episode for you to watch in the watch list section of the user home page. You can see below that you can mark the episode as watched by swiping it away or by using the toggle switch. To refresh the list after marking episodes as watched you can pull the list down to refresh. To go to the show page, click on the show poster.

My Account

This page allows you to change your profile picture and sign out. To change the profile picture, tap the current picture and it will first ask for storage permissions. Then it will launch the file chooser. Underneath the sign out button, there is a sentence that tells you how many minutes of T.V. you have marked as watched.
